Coon Hunter Cake

Description

Fairly easy to make, and feel free to improvise this Coon Hunter Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Yellow Cake Mixcan also use sugar free cake mix
  • 1 Large Can Crushed Pineapples
  • 1 Cup Crushed Pecans
  • 1 Cup Coconut
  • 1 Stick Margarine
  • 1 8oz Pack of Cream Cheese

Steps Cake

  1. Mix cake mix as directed on box
  2. Add pineapple, coconut, and pecans to cake mix
  3. Bake on 350 degrees until golden brown

Steps Icing

  1. Mix all ingredients well
  2. Add milk if needed
