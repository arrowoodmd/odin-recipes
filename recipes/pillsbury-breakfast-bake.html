Pillsbury Breakfast Bake

Description

Nice hardy breakfast for the family

Ingredients

  • 1 Package of 12oz **** Pork Sausage
  • 1/3 Cup Chopped Onions
  • 1/3 Cup Chopped Green Bell Peppers
  • 1/3 Cup Chopped Red Bell Peppers
  • 10 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Can 16.3oz Pillsbury Grandes Flaky Layer Biscuits

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Spray a 9x13 glass baking dish with cooking spray (A different sized dish will work as long as it's a similar size)
  3. In a 10" skillet brown the sausage, onions and bell peppers. Drain well
  4. In a large bowl; beat the eggs
  5. Stir in cheese as well as the sausage mixture in with the eggs
  6. Seperate dough into 8 biscuits and press into the bottom of the baking dish
  7. Pour sausage mixture over the biscuits
  8. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until egg mixture is set and crust is a deep golden brown
  9. Cool 5 minutes before serving
