Blueberry Muffins

Description

Fun breakfast muffins for the family. Recipe makes 12 muffins.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 Cup Milk
  • 1/4 Cup Oil
  • 1-1/2 Cups Plain Flour
  • 1/2 Cup Sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons Baking Powder
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Salt
  • 1 Cup Fresh Blueberries

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Mix all the ingredients
  3. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes
